## Recommended Entry / Exit Framework
- Preferred setup: `pullback_100_10` because it kept drawdown below 35%, produced at least 10 trades, and had the strongest risk-adjusted profile among the robust candidates.
- Conservative variant: `pullback_100_10` if you want the lower-drawdown version of the same pullback idea.
- MA baseline: `ma_50_200` is the best of the tested crossover set, but the pullback family was more aligned with swing-style entries in NVDA.

### Entry Rules
- Use the `100-day` SMA as the trend filter. Long setups are valid only when the daily close is above that trend line.
- Confirm short-term momentum with `8 EMA > 21 EMA`.
- Wait for a pullback toward the `10-day` SMA. The prior close should be within roughly 1% of that pullback average.
- Enter only after the next daily close resumes higher: price must close back above the pullback average and above the prior close.
- Do not force entries when the latest signal is `none`; the rule set is designed to wait for pullbacks, not chase strength.

### Exit Rules
- Exit when the daily close breaks below the `100-day` SMA.
- Exit when `8 EMA < 21 EMA` because that indicates the short-term trend has rolled over.
- If neither exit condition has triggered, stay in the trade and let the trend continue. This is a swing framework, not a fast-profit scalper.
